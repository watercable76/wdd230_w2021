Idaho is such a diverse place, rich with forests, a variety of outdoor activities, large open areas and beautiful sunsets. All of these events are available in each part of the state, and some parts of the state offer all of these activities.


Some great places to visit include Fish Haven, Soda Springs and Preston. All of these cities are very friendly and have so much to do! If you would like to know more about all of these places, visit the links above, which will bring you to a site with more information.


Another important thing to notice are the Stormcenter and Gallerysite links. If you are in an emergency storm situation, go to the Stormcenter page and enter your information. This will let us know the level of danger you are in, as well as where and who you are. The Gallery page contains images of Idaho, and can only show a glimpse into the beauty of this state.
