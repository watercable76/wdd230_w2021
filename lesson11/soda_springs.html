A Day at The Park

Kelly Park

Kelly Park is over 20 acres in size, and offers many different activities for all who come. There are various softball fields, tennis courts, playgrounds, and even several kilometers of walking trails.

Soda Springs Bike Park

Soda Springs bike park was funded by pure donations, and is suited for all riders. There are easy, intermediate, advanced and skilled trails. Ample parking is available towards the entrance of the park, as well as picnic areas. Riders who go to this park are expected to live up to basic standards and be courteous to all other riders.
