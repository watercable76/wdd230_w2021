Come See It All

If you are looking for a city with great outdoor activities, this is the place for you! In Fish Haven, there is bird watching, historic monuments, and even the great Bear Lake!

Bird Watching

In the Bear Lake National Wildlife Refuge, there are many species of birds to find. This refuge is over 1,700 acres in size, and is home to sandhill cranes, herons, and many species of geese and ducks.

Historic Monuments

The rich culture in this area offers great insights into the past of Idaho. The pioneer ancestry and influence of the railroads changed the course of Idaho and determined some cultural aspects that we see to this day.

The Great Bear Lake

The vast lake that spans both Idaho and Utah is great for all who come to see it. Whether it be fishing, skating on the frozen lake, or just going out for a swim, this lake has it all!
